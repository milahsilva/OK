🎀 Brand Story

✨ Como tudo começou

Sempre tive uma conexão especial com crianças. Desde cedo, cuidar, ensinar e acompanhar o desenvolvimento delas se tornou algo natural para mim.

💖 Por que decidi ser babá

Escolhi essa profissão porque acredito no poder do carinho, da segurança e da presença afetiva na infância. Ser babá é mais do que um trabalho — é uma missão.

🌟 Minha trajetória

Ao longo do tempo, fui aprimorando minhas habilidades, estudando, aprendendo com cada família e construindo uma forma única de cuidar: com amor, responsabilidade e respeito.

This is the essence of my brand — and it's a pleasure to share it with you.

💡Tips for Parents

🏡 How to Prepare the Home

  • Keep the environment safe and well‑organized.
  • Remove any hazardous objects from the child’s reach.
  • Prepare a comfortable and secure space for play.

🎒 What to Set Aside

  • Fraldas, lenços e pomada
  • A set of extra clothes
  • Alimentos e mamadeiras
  • Remédios autorizados (se necessário)

💞 How to Make the Adjustment Easier

  • Explain the child’s routine
  • Show where the essential items are kept
  • Keep a comfort object nearby
  • Say goodbye with warmth and reassurance

🧩 Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some quick answers to common questions.

💳 How does payment work?
Payments are made exclusively through Vipps. For monthly packages, payment is completed at the beginning of the month.
📅 How far in advance should I book?
For regular daytime appointments, booking in advance is recommended. For special nighttime appointments, please contact me directly with the date and time.
🛑 Do you offer last‑minute bookings?
Last‑minute bookings may be available depending on my schedule. Please contact me to check availability.
⭐ Cancellations and rescheduling
Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance. Rescheduling can be done at no additional cost.

Note: For any additional questions, feel free to contact me and I will be happy to assist you.
